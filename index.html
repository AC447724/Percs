The domain percs.fun and all associated infrastructure has been seized by the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) and the United States Department of Justice pursuant to a court order issued by the United States District Court for the District of Columbia.

This seizure is part of an ongoing joint law enforcement operation targeting federal crimes including wire fraud, money laundering, and conspiracy. Evidence obtained from this domain is being analyzed by federal authorities.

Individuals with information related to this investigation are encouraged to contact the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3) at ic3.gov or call 1-800-CALL-FBI.
